# Constitution

This document is the canonical entry point for Yini's product constitution.
It summarizes the permanent decisions that guide implementation and links to the
source documents in `specs/`.

## Purpose

Yini is an internal assistant for senior insurance advisors. The product must
help them retrieve, analyze, compare, and summarize official Sura insurance
documents with strong grounding, traceability, and human oversight.

## Constitution Parts

- [Mission](specs/mission.md)
- [Tech Stack](specs/tech-stack.md)
- [Roadmap](specs/roadmap.md)

## Operating Rules

- `PRD.md` remains the source of truth for requirements.
- The constitution defines durable product and engineering decisions.
- Implementation work should follow the roadmap in `specs/roadmap.md`.
- New specs should extend this foundation rather than contradict it.
